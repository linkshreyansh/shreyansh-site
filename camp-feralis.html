Camp Feralis

by Shreyansh Sahoo

Book Description

Welcome to Camp Feralis—where summer fun turns into a fight for survival.

When Shrey arrives at what's supposed to be the camp of his dreams, he quickly realizes something is terribly wrong. Kids are screaming. Animals are attacking without warning. And the adults who promised to protect them seem to have a dark secret.

As danger closes in from every direction, Shrey and his friends must race against time to uncover the truth behind Camp Feralis. Strange occurrences escalate into life-threatening situations, and trust becomes a luxury they can't afford. Every shadow hides a potential threat. Every answer leads to darker questions.

But the real terror isn't just surviving the camp—it's discovering what the counselors have been hiding all along. Will Shrey and his friends solve the mystery before it's too late, or will they become the next victims of Camp Feralis? One thing is certain—at Camp Feralis, the nightmare has only just begun. Perfect for fans of thrilling adventures and spine-tingling mysteries, this page-turner will keep young readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.
