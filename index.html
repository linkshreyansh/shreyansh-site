What's Your Story?

What's Your Story? : My First 50 Pages—is an ultimate story journal for young minds to capture their happiest moments, cherished memories, and proudest achievements! This book captures the author's treasured moments and innovative ideas while offering a journal section on each page to document your child's thoughts and memorable experiences. Packed with accomplishments, stories, and fun activities, this journal encourages kids to reflect on their daily lives while nurturing positive habits. It’s designed to spark imagination, boost self-esteem, and help children embrace gratitude in a way they’ll love and look forward to every day. Order "What's Your Story? My First 50 Pages" today and empower your child with a gift of joy, mindfulness, and self-discovery! This book is a reminder that everyone has a story to tell and creating a book is one of the best ways to cherish those memories forever. Through this book Shreyansh - The author hopes to encourage his peers to explore their creativity, dreams, and see their achievements as milestones in their life journey.

Camp Feralis

Welcome to Camp Feralis—where summer fun turns into a fight for survival. When Shrey arrives at what's supposed to be the camp of his dreams, he quickly realizes something is terribly wrong. Kids are screaming. Animals are attacking without warning. And the adults who promised to protect them seem to have a dark secret. As danger closes in from every direction, Shrey and his friends must race against time to uncover the truth behind Camp Feralis. Strange occurrences escalate into life-threatening situations, and trust becomes a luxury they can't afford. Every shadow hides a potential threat. Every answer leads to darker questions. But the real terror isn't just surviving the camp—it's discovering what the counselors have been hiding all along. Will Shrey and his friends solve the mystery before it's too late, or will they become the next victims of Camp Feralis? One thing is certain—at Camp Feralis, the nightmare has only just begun. Perfect for fans of thrilling adventures and spine-tingling mysteries, this page-turner will keep young readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.
